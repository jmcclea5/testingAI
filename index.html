About Smile Every Mile

Founded in October 2024, Smile Every Mile is a group of fitness-minded individuals working to make a difference in our communities of Quincy and Dorchester. We promote health and wellness through sporting events like road races, bike rides, clinics, and tournaments.

All proceeds from our events go to our Good Neighbor Fund, which supports families in need across Dorchester and Quincy.
